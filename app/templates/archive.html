▶ What happens when you import — one click runs the whole pipeline, in order
  1. 1
    Download your mail
    Saves new messages as .eml from the folders you tick. Read-only on the server — nothing is moved, deleted, or marked read — and it auto-resumes if the connection drops, skipping anything already imported.
  2. 2
    Re-stitch threads
    Rebuilds the conversation index so replies and forwards group into one thread.
  3. 3
    Fetch share links & attachments download is optional
    Catalogues the share links in new mail, and — if you tick “download share links” below — downloads the files (SharePoint/OneDrive, Drive, Dropbox, WeTransfer, Box) first, so the next step can read them. Very large files pause for your confirmation in Mail.
  4. 4
    Comprehend each thread auto · budget-capped
    The AI reads each new thread — including downloaded attachments — into one place. This single step produces the category (→ Hub grouping & project), the people on it (→ Rolodex), and your to-dos & flagged issues (→ Matters). Tune model/speed/timeout in /dev; skipped if auto-comprehend is off.
  5. 5
    Refresh the Rolodex
    Pulls names, titles, and phone numbers from email signatures into your contacts directory.
