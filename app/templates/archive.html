▶ What happens when you archive — one click runs the whole pipeline, in order
  1. 1
    Download your mail
    Saves new messages as .eml from the folders you tick. Read-only on the server — nothing is moved, deleted, or marked read — and it auto-resumes if the connection drops, skipping anything already archived.
  2. 2
    Re-stitch threads
    Rebuilds the conversation index so replies and forwards group into one thread.
  3. 3
    Comprehend new threads auto · budget-capped
    The AI reads each new thread into a summary, key facts, and cited to-dos. Controlled in /dev (model, parallelism, timeout); skipped if auto-comprehend is off.
  4. 4
    Refresh the GreenBook
    Pulls names, titles, and phone numbers from email signatures into your contacts directory.
  5. 5
    Catalogue share links & attachments download is optional
    Always lists the share links found in new mail. If you tick “download share links” below, it also fetches the files (SharePoint/OneDrive, Drive, Dropbox, WeTransfer, Box) — very large files pause for your confirmation in Mail.
